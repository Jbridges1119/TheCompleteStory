GET   /login            ~REQUEST LOGIN PAGE -- STRETCH
GET   /login/:id        ~REQUEST USER LOGGED IN COOKIE

GET   /                   ~REDIRECT TO /STORIES
GET   /STORIES            ~REQUEST STORY LIST PAGE
GET   /STORIES/NEW        ~REQUEST CREATE STORY PAGE
GET   /STORIES/:ID        ~REQUEST SPECIFIC STORY PAGE

POST  /STORIES            ~CREATE NEW STORY
POST  /STORIES/:ID        ~CREATE NEW CONTRIBUTION

GET   /USERS              ~REQUEST OWNER STORY LIST PAGE
GET   /USERS/:ID          ~REQUEST OWNER INDIVIDUAL STORY
POST  /USERS/:ID          ~ADD SPECIFIC CONTRIBUTION AND CONTINUE



POST  /users/end/:id      ~ADD SPECIFIC CONTRIBUTION AND END
POST  /users/like/:id     ~ADD 1 INTEGER TO RATING AND SHOW NUMBER - AJAX
POST  /users/dilike/:id   ~REMOVE 1 INTEGER FROM RATING AND SHOW NUMBER -AJAX
